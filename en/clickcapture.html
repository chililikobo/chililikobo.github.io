Product Overview

"Everything you need, captured just by clicking."

ClickCapture is a background utility that automatically captures and saves screenshots in sync with your mouse's "left-click" actions. Whether you are creating operation manuals or recording parameter settings during software installations, you can record your entire workflow as images without any conscious effort.

Click-Synced Capture

Automatically saves the target window or entire screen as an image every time you click.

Smart Duplicate Filtering

Intelligently skips saving if the screen remains unchanged, preventing your folders from overflowing with identical images.

Instant ON/OFF via Hotkey

If you no longer need to capture during your work, simply press the assigned hotkey ("F8" or "F9") to instantly toggle the capture feature ON or OFF (Default is "F9").

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. No images are being saved when I click. Is it working properly?

A. Please check if the "Skip duplicate images" setting is enabled.
When active, the app intelligently skips saving if the screen content has not changed since the last capture to prevent duplicate files. Try disabling this setting temporarily to verify if it captures normally.

Q. Why does the application require Administrator privileges at startup?

A. This is necessary to monitor click interactions globally across other applications (Global Hook).
Due to Windows security specifications, standard low-privilege apps cannot accurately monitor inputs of other apps, making admin access essential to fully utilize this tool.

Q. My antivirus software flagged the utility as suspicious.

A. Because the application utilizes techniques to monitor mouse operations (hooks), certain antivirus programs may incorrectly flag it as a false positive.
If you are running the software in a trusted environment, please add the application to your security software's exclusion list.

Q. The boundaries of the captured screenshots seem misaligned.

A. This issue can happen if your Windows "Display Scale" (DPI scaling) is configured to a value other than 100% (e.g., 125% or 150%).
If you have changed your display scaling settings, please close the app and restart it to apply the correct scaling.
