Product Description

"The information you need, when you need it, at maximum speed."

Ohnuki-san Lite is a free software specialized in displaying essential information for daily PC management, maintaining the same ease of use as the "Plus" edition.

No Installation Required

Runs from a single executable without touching the registry. It’s fully portable—just carry it on a USB drive.

Intuitive UI

No complex setup required. View all specs and serial numbers on a single screen the moment you launch it.

Regarding Domain Environments

Ohnuki-san Lite is designed for personal use and small workgroup environments. Operation in domain environments (Active Directory, etc.) is not supported.

If you need to bulk collect and manage information for corporate IT asset management or PC kitting of domain-joined devices, please use the premium version, Ohnuki-san Plus.
