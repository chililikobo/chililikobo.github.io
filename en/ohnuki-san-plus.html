Licensing & Multi-Device Use

Ohnuki-san Plus features a "Slave Mode" designed for flexible field operations.

Slave Mode (Multi-Device Use)

With a single license, you can use the software on offline "Slave" devices in addition to your primary "Master" machine.

Slave Device Restrictions
Continuous use is limited to 30 days. After this, "license re-registration" or a "Master update" is required to continue.

How to Refresh the Usage Period

1 Connect your Master PC to the internet and complete license authentication.
2 Once authenticated, copy the entire "Ohnuki-san Plus" folder from the Master machine.
3 Overwrite the files on the Slave device to refresh the usage period.
